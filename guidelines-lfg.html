GUIDELINES

[KOHI] Runs are fun and chill, never elitist. Led by official raid and strike commanders only.
If hosting or participating in a [KOHI] Run, you must represent [KOHI] and follow these guidelines:

  • Do not be an elitist. Even EXP players may have different gaming skills and not meet your expectations. Go call out other players elsewhere.
  • Everyone must be in the corresponding voice channel in Discord for proper communication.
  • Everyone must have fun.
