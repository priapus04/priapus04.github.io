Trainers will create a thread in the #raids or #strikes channel to open the signing up for a batch training. The sign-up will be open for a maximum of one week. If either one week has passed or five members have already signed up, the sign-up will be closed. The trainees and trainer will then decide on the schedule of their training based on their availability. They can either opt to use; 
https://when2meet.com/ or talk amongst themselves in the thread.
